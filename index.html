You have one simple mission: get Chad's science homework from your house to his. The city is full of eyes, vibes, and accidental social contact.

Start The Walk

World Overview

This is a top-down city run where anxiety is the real boss fight. People can notice you if they look at you, drift too close, or catch behavior that feels off. Walls are your cover and your breather spots.

How To Play

Move

WASD to move through the city.

Hoodie

I toggles hoodie up/down to lower how obvious you feel.

Phone

O toggles phone out/away to blend in while moving slower.

Wall-Flower

P near a wall to stop, lean back, and decompress.

Win

Reach Chad's house with anxiety below max.

Lose

If anxiety reaches 100, you are overwhelmed and must try again.
